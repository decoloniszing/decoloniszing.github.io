The Zing




Unfortunately, search engine algorithms make it very difficult for underrepresented narratives to be seen or heard. We want to actively tackle this and make it easier for these narratives to come to the surface.

The Zing is a monthly curatorial newsletter by team dob dedicated to celebrating and promoting art and knowledge by people of historically marginalised and intersectional identities.
